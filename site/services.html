Comprehensive Life Insurance Options

As an independent broker, I work with multiple top carriers to find you the best coverage at the most competitive rate. Here's what I offer.

Term Life Insurance

Term life insurance provides pure protection for a set period — typically 10, 20, or 30 years. It's the most affordable way to get a large amount of coverage and is ideal for families with specific time-bound financial obligations.

  • Most affordable option per dollar of coverage
  • Flexible term lengths (10, 15, 20, 25, 30 years)
  • Level premiums — your rate stays the same
  • Convertible to permanent coverage
  • Coverage amounts from $50K to $10M+

Who Is Term Life Best For?
Homeowners

Cover your mortgage so your family keeps the home if something happens to you.

Young Parents

Protect your children's future until they're financially independent.

Income Earners

Replace your income during your peak earning years.

Parents Saving for College

Ensure education funds are available for your children.

Whole Life Insurance

Whole life insurance provides lifelong coverage with a guaranteed death benefit and builds cash value over time. It's a cornerstone of long-term financial planning that combines protection with savings.

  • Coverage that lasts your entire life
  • Guaranteed cash value accumulation
  • Fixed premiums that never increase
  • Tax-advantaged cash value growth
  • Option to borrow against your policy
Get a Whole Life Quote
Who Is Whole Life Best For?
Long-Term Planners

Build cash value while maintaining permanent coverage for your family.

Estate Planners

Leave a guaranteed legacy and help cover estate taxes.

Business Owners

Fund buy-sell agreements or key person coverage.

Conservative Savers

Guaranteed growth with no market risk on cash value.

Final Expense Insurance

Final expense insurance is a smaller whole life policy designed specifically to cover end-of-life costs. It helps ensure your family isn't burdened with funeral expenses, medical bills, or outstanding debts.

  • Coverage typically $5,000 – $50,000
  • Simplified underwriting — fewer health questions
  • No medical exam required
  • Affordable premiums for seniors
  • Benefits paid directly to beneficiary
Get a Final Expense Quote
Who Is Final Expense Best For?
Seniors (50–85)

Affordable coverage designed specifically for your age group.

Those With Health Conditions

Simplified issue means more people qualify, even with pre-existing conditions.

Considerate Parents

Don't leave funeral costs to your children. The average funeral costs $7,000–$12,000.

Debt Holders

Cover remaining debts so they don't pass to your estate.

Indexed Universal Life (IUL)

IUL combines the flexibility of universal life with growth potential linked to a stock market index. It offers a floor of protection against losses while allowing your cash value to participate in market gains.

  • Flexible premiums and death benefits
  • Cash value tied to index performance (S&P 500, etc.)
  • Downside protection — 0% floor means you never lose to market drops
  • Tax-free retirement income potential
  • Living benefits for chronic/critical illness

Who Is IUL Best For?
Growth-Minded Individuals

Want upside potential without risking your principal.

Retirement Planners

Create a supplemental tax-free income stream for retirement.

High-Income Earners

Already maxing out 401k/IRA? IUL offers additional tax-advantaged savings.

Balanced Investors

Love the idea of market participation with a guaranteed floor.
