About Me

Hi, I'm Mitch Van Dusen, a 30-year-old husband and father dedicated to helping Texas families secure their financial future through life insurance.

I moved to Texas in 2020 with my wife Madasyn and have since fallen in love with calling this great state home. We're raising our two young children here, a spirited 3-year-old and an energetic 1-year-old, and that personal experience drives everything I do professionally.

Having two little ones of my own has given me a deep understanding of just how important it is to protect the people you love most. For the past 5 years, I've made it my mission to guide clients through the process with care, detail, and a true client-first approach. I take the time to listen, educate, and find solutions that fit each family's unique needs and budget. I never push, I always support.

When I'm not working with clients, you'll likely find me on the ice. I'm a passionate hockey player and proud goalie. The teamwork, focus, and resilience required in the net translate directly to how I serve my clients. I'm always ready, always protecting what matters.

I'd love the opportunity to earn your trust and help protect your family's tomorrow. Feel free to reach out anytime.
