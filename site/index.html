About Me

Hi, I'm Mitch Van Dusen, a 30-year-old husband and father dedicated to helping Texas families secure their financial future through life insurance.

Originally born and raised in Alberta, Canada I moved to Texas in 2020 with my wife Madasyn and have since fallen in love with calling this great state home. We're raising our two young children here, a spirited 3-year-old and an energetic 1-year-old. That personal experience as a young father drives everything I do professionally and gives me unique insight into the concerns and priorities most families face today.

Having two little ones of my own has given me a deep understanding of just how important it is to protect the people you love most. For the past 5 years, I've made it my mission to guide clients through the often-confusing world of life insurance with care, honesty, and a true client-first approach.

I take the time to truly listen to your situation, educate you on all available options, and find solutions that fit both your family's unique needs and your budget. I never use high-pressure tactics. My goal is simple: help you feel confident and protected, knowing your loved ones will be taken care of no matter what.

When I'm not working with clients, you'll likely find me on the ice. I'm a passionate hockey player and proud goalie. The teamwork, focus, and resilience required in the net translate directly to how I serve my clients: I'm always ready, always protecting what matters most.

I'd love the opportunity to earn your trust and help protect your family's tomorrow. Feel free to reach out anytime for a no-obligation conversation.
