My Story

From Alberta to America

Hi, I'm Mitch Van Dusen, a 30-year-old husband and father dedicated to helping families across the country secure their financial future through life insurance.

Originally born and raised in Alberta, Canada, I moved to the United States in 2020 with my wife Madasyn. We're raising our two young children together — a spirited 3-year-old and an energetic 1-year-old.

What started as a personal need to protect my own growing family quickly turned into a passion for helping others do the same. I believe every family deserves clear, honest guidance when it comes to life insurance — and that's exactly what I provide.
